\section{Risk Category Mapping}

Based on Loi 18-07 (amended by Loi 25-11):

\begin{longtable}{|p{3cm}|p{7cm}|p{5.5cm}|}
\hline
\textbf{Category} & \textbf{Entities} & \textbf{Action} \\
\hline
\endhead

\textcolor{catI}{\textbf{I --- Direct Identifiers}}
&
PERSON, NIN, EMAIL, PHONE, PASSPORT, RIB, RC, NIF, Signature
&
Mandatory removal before LLM
\\ \hline

\textcolor{catII}{\textbf{II --- Quasi-Identifiers}}
&
DOB, AGE, PROFESSION, SALARY, ADDRESS (partial), ZIP, COMPANY
&
k-anonymize (cohort $\geq$ 5)
\\ \hline

\textcolor{catIII}{\textbf{III --- Sensitive}}
&
HEALTH, DIAGNOSIS, GENETIC, RELIGION, POLITICS
&
Absolute block --- reject document
\\ \hline

\end{longtable}

\section{Implementation Priority}

Based on document frequency in Algerian enterprise archiving:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Identity Docs} (CIN, Passport, Birth Certificate) --- highest frequency
    \item \textbf{Contracts \& Invoices} --- core business documents
    \item \textbf{Payslips} --- common HR archive items
    \item \textbf{Medical Reports} --- highest risk (Category III)
    \item \textbf{Bank Statements} --- financial privacy
    \item \textbf{Diplomas \& CVs} --- recruitment archives
    \item \textbf{Court Judgments} --- legal archives
    \item \textbf{Property Titles} --- real estate archives
\end{enumerate}
